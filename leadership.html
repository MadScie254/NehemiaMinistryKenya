Senior Leadership

God's chosen vessels who carry the vision, provide strategic direction, and shepherd our church family with apostolic authority and pastoral heart

Pastor Samuel Wanjala

Senior Pastor & Founder

Called by God in 1998 during a powerful encounter while working as a teacher in rural Kakamega, Pastor Samuel left his secure government position to plant what would become one of Kenya's most influential churches. His journey from skeptical educator to passionate evangelist demonstrates God's transformative power.

With a Master's in Theology from Nairobi International School of Theology and over 25 years of ministry experience, Pastor Samuel carries a unique anointing for church planting, leadership development, and community transformation. Under his leadership, Nehemia Ministry has planted 47 churches across East Africa and impacted over 50,000 lives.

His heart for the lost, passion for discipleship, and unwavering commitment to biblical truth have made him a respected voice in Kenya's church community. Married to Grace for 22 years, they have four children who actively serve in ministry.

Pastor Grace Wanjala

Co-Pastor & Women's Ministry Leader

A woman of extraordinary faith and prophetic insight, Pastor Grace has been instrumental in shaping the spiritual culture of Nehemia Ministry. Her journey began as a young mother seeking God's will for her family, which led to a radical encounter with Jesus that transformed her entire worldview.

With specialized training in counseling and women's ministry from Africa Leadership University, Pastor Grace leads our thriving women's ministry that has empowered over 3,000 women across Western Kenya. Her compassionate heart and wisdom have made her a sought-after speaker and mentor for women throughout East Africa.

Known for her gift of intercession and prophetic ministry, Pastor Grace carries a special anointing for healing and deliverance. Her weekly women's meetings consistently see miraculous breakthroughs, restored marriages, and transformed lives. She is a living testimony that God uses ordinary people to accomplish extraordinary things.

Our Leadership Values

These core values guide every decision, shape every interaction, and define the character we pursue as leaders in God's house

Integrity

We lead with authenticity, honesty, and moral excellence, ensuring our private lives match our public ministry.

Servanthood

Following Jesus' example, we lead by serving others, prioritizing their growth and wellbeing above our own comfort.

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in all we do, believing that God deserves our very best effort and dedication.

Growth

We commit to continuous learning, spiritual development, and personal transformation as we lead others in their journey.

Vision

We keep our eyes fixed on God's eternal purpose, making decisions that advance His Kingdom beyond our generation.

Unity

We work together as one team, celebrating our diversity while maintaining our commitment to shared vision and values.

Our Leadership Vision

"To raise up a generation of Kingdom leaders who will transform nations through the power of the Gospel, establishing churches that become centers of spiritual revival, community transformation, and global missions."

We believe that effective leadership begins with a deep relationship with Jesus Christ and manifests through:

  • Character Development: Building leaders whose integrity matches their influence
  • Spiritual Formation: Cultivating intimacy with God as the foundation of all ministry
  • Practical Training: Equipping leaders with skills, knowledge, and wisdom for effective service
  • Mentorship: Creating relational environments where leaders are nurtured and developed
  • Global Perspective: Preparing leaders to think beyond local ministry to worldwide impact

Our commitment is to model the leadership we want to see reproduced in others, creating a multiplication effect that will impact generations to come. We measure our success not by the size of our own ministry, but by the number of effective leaders we release into God's harvest field.
