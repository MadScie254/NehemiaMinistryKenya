Core Ministries

These foundational ministries form the heartbeat of our church, creating spaces where every person can encounter God, grow in their faith, and discover their divine purpose

Worship Ministry

Experience Heaven on Earth through our anointed worship services. Our talented team of musicians, singers, and worship leaders create an atmosphere where the presence of God is tangible, hearts are opened, and lives are transformed through authentic praise and worship.

What We Offer:

  • • Contemporary and traditional worship styles
  • • Music training and development programs
  • • Special worship nights and concerts
  • • Youth and children's worship teams

Meeting Times: Sundays 9:00 AM & 11:30 AM

Youth Ministry

Igniting passion for Jesus in the hearts of young people aged 13-25. Our dynamic youth ministry combines relevant teaching, exciting activities, leadership development, and authentic community to help young people navigate life's challenges while building an unshakeable foundation in Christ.

Programs Include:

  • • Weekly youth services with relevant teaching
  • • Annual youth conferences and retreats
  • • Community service and outreach projects
  • • Leadership training and mentorship

Meeting Times: Fridays 6:00 PM, Sundays 2:00 PM

Women's Ministry

Empowering women to embrace their identity in Christ and fulfill their divine destiny. Through Bible studies, fellowship gatherings, mentorship programs, and community outreach, we create a nurturing environment where women can grow spiritually, build meaningful relationships, and discover their unique calling.

Ministry Highlights:

  • • Weekly Bible studies and prayer meetings
  • • Marriage and family enrichment seminars
  • • Women's conferences and retreats
  • • Skills training and empowerment programs

Meeting Times: Thursdays 2:00 PM, Saturdays 10:00 AM

Children's Ministry

Building strong foundations in young hearts through age-appropriate teaching, engaging activities, and loving care. Our children's ministry serves ages 2-12, providing a safe and nurturing environment where children can learn about Jesus, develop godly character, and experience God's love through dedicated teachers and volunteers.

Age Groups:

  • • Nursery (2-3 years) - Basic Bible stories and songs
  • • Preschool (4-5 years) - Interactive learning and play
  • • Elementary (6-12 years) - Scripture memory and character building
  • • Special events and children's church productions

Meeting Times: Sundays 9:00 AM & 11:30 AM

Community Outreach

Demonstrating God's love through practical action in our community. Our comprehensive outreach programs address hunger, healthcare, education, and spiritual needs throughout Kakamega County and beyond, serving as the hands and feet of Jesus to those who need hope and healing most.

Outreach Programs:

  • • Weekly feeding program (850+ children)
  • • Free medical clinic and health services
  • • Educational scholarships and school supplies
  • • Clean water projects and well drilling

Volunteer Days: Wednesdays & Saturdays

Missions Ministry

Taking the Gospel to the ends of the earth through strategic church planting, cross-cultural missions, and leadership development. Our missions ministry has established 47 churches across East Africa and continues to train indigenous leaders who will multiply the Kingdom impact for generations to come.

Mission Fields:

  • • Kenya: 32 church plants in rural communities
  • • Uganda: 8 congregations and training centers
  • • Tanzania: 7 churches and orphan care ministry
  • • Leadership training and Bible schools

Training: Monthly missions preparation classes

Ministry Impact Stories

Witness the incredible transformation happening through our ministries as lives are touched, communities are changed, and the Kingdom of God advances

Educational Transformation

Through Our Children's Ministry

"When Mary Wanjiku first brought her three children to our church, they couldn't afford school fees and the children had been out of school for two years. Through our educational scholarship program and children's ministry support, all three children are now enrolled in school. Her eldest daughter recently graduated top of her class and received a scholarship to secondary school. Mary says, 'Nehemia Ministry didn't just help my children get an education – they gave my family hope for the future.'"

120 scholarships provided this year 95% graduation rate among recipients

Healthcare Miracle

Through Our Medical Outreach

"Pastor James discovered 8-year-old Samuel collapsed by the roadside, severely malnourished and fighting malaria. Through our medical clinic, Samuel received immediate treatment and ongoing care. Today, three months later, Samuel is healthy, attending school, and actively participating in our children's ministry. His grandmother testifies, 'This church saved my grandson's life. We thought we had lost him, but God used Nehemia Ministry to bring him back to us.'"

1,500 patients treated this year Free medical care for all services

Spiritual Breakthrough

Through Our Youth Ministry

"Rebecca came to youth ministry as a rebellious teenager struggling with substance abuse and family conflicts. Through consistent mentoring, prayer support, and the love of her youth leaders, she surrendered her life to Christ. Now 19, Rebecca leads our peer counseling program and has helped over 20 other young people overcome similar struggles. She declares, 'This ministry didn't just change my life – it gave me a purpose worth living for.'"

200+ youth served annually 50+ life transformations documented

Church Planting Success

Through Our Missions Ministry

"In the remote village of Kitale, Pastor David planted a church with just five families in 2022. Through our missions support, training, and resources, this small congregation has grown to over 150 members. They've established a primary school, drilled a water well, and started three satellite churches in neighboring villages. Pastor David reports, 'The entire region has been transformed by the Gospel. What seemed impossible became reality through God's power and Nehemia's partnership.'"

47 churches planted to date 8,000+ souls reached through church plants
