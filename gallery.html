Years of Memories

Sunday Morning Worship

Our congregation raises their voices in powerful praise and worship, creating an atmosphere where Heaven touches Earth.

Worship Team Leading Praise

Our talented worship team leads the congregation into the presence of God through anointed music and heartfelt worship.

Baptism Celebration

A powerful moment as new believers publicly declare their faith in Jesus Christ through water baptism.

River Baptism

Our annual river baptism service where dozens of new converts are baptized in the flowing waters, symbolizing their new life in Christ.

Feeding the Hungry

Our weekly feeding program provides nutritious meals to over 850 children and families in need throughout Kakamega County.

Medical Mission

Our free medical clinic serves the community with professional healthcare services, bringing healing and hope to those who cannot afford treatment.

Youth Conference

Our annual youth conference brings together hundreds of young people for powerful worship, life-changing messages, and spiritual breakthrough.

Youth Bible Study

Weekly youth Bible studies where young believers dive deep into God's Word and build lasting friendships rooted in faith.

Christmas Celebration

Our annual Christmas celebration fills the sanctuary with joy as we commemorate the birth of our Savior with music, drama, and fellowship.

Church Anniversary

Celebrating another year of God's faithfulness as we reflect on His goodness and look forward to greater things ahead.

Prayer and Worship

Bringing clean water to rural villages through our well-drilling ministry, transforming entire communities with this basic necessity of life.

Wedding Ceremony

Stories Behind the Photos

Every image captures a moment of transformation, a testimony of God's goodness, and a story of lives changed forever

Sarah Mwangi

Baptized December 2023

"When I see the photo of my baptism, I remember the overwhelming peace I felt as I went under the water. That moment marked the end of my old life and the beginning of everything beautiful God had planned for me. Today, I serve as a Sunday school teacher, pouring into the next generation what was poured into me."

Peter Kimani

Outreach Coordinator

"Every photo from our outreach programs tells a story of hope restored. I've seen children smile for the first time in months, mothers weep with gratitude, and entire families transformed by a simple act of love. These images remind me that we're not just feeding bodies – we're nourishing souls."
