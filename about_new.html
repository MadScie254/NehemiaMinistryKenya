The Genesis of Our Ministry

Every great ministry begins with a divine encounter, and ours is no exception. This is the remarkable story of how God raised up Nehemia Ministry Kenya.

A Divine Calling Unfolds

In 1991, in the humble village of Mukhonje, Kakamega County, a young man named David Walukhu experienced a life-changing encounter with God. Having grown up in a family where faith was central, David felt the unmistakable call of the Holy Spirit to minister to his people. What began as small prayer meetings in his family home would eventually grow into one of the most influential ministries in Western Kenya.

The name "Nehemia" was not chosen by chance. Just as Nehemia in the Bible was called to rebuild the walls of Jerusalem, our ministry was established with the divine mandate to rebuild broken lives, restore families, and reconstruct communities that had been devastated by poverty, spiritual darkness, and hopelessness.

From Humble Beginnings to Mighty Works

The early days were marked by incredible faith and miraculous provision. Pastor David, along with his beloved wife Selina, began their ministry with nothing but unwavering faith and a deep love for souls. They conducted services under trees, in borrowed halls, and in their own home, never imagining that God would one day give them a beautiful sanctuary that would host thousands of worshippers.

Through decades of faithful service, countless souls have been saved, families have been restored, the sick have been healed, and the community has been transformed. Today, Nehemia Ministry Kenya stands as a testament to God's faithfulness and the power of persistent, faith-filled ministry.

Milestones of Faith

A journey through the significant moments that shaped our ministry and touched countless lives

1991

The Divine Call

Pastor David Walukhu receives a powerful vision from God to establish a ministry that would rebuild lives and restore hope. The first prayer meetings begin in his family home with just five faithful believers.

1995

First Sanctuary Built

Through miraculous provision and community support, the first church building is constructed. The congregation grows to over 100 faithful members, and the first healing miracles are documented.

2000

Community Outreach Begins

The ministry launches its first community programs including a feeding program for orphans, a medical clinic, and educational scholarships for underprivileged children.

2010

Regional Recognition

Pastor David is ordained as Bishop by the International Association of Ministers. The ministry expands to include three satellite churches and a Bible college.

2020

Digital Ministry Launch

Adapting to global changes, the ministry launches online services, digital prayer walls, and virtual conferences, reaching believers across Africa and beyond.

2025

Global Vision Expansion

Today, we serve over 5,000 active members, operate 12 community programs, and have planted churches in 8 countries across East Africa, continuing to rebuild lives globally.
