Our Enduring Journey of Faith and Service

Step into the narrative of Nehemia Ministry Kenya, a testament to God's faithfulness and the power of a vision pursued with unwavering dedication.

Nehemia Ministry Kenya, joyfully established in the year 2006 under the divine guidance and visionary leadership of Bishop David Walukhu and his devoted wife, Reverend Selina Walukhu, first took root as a humble, yet profoundly spirit-led grassroots movement. Its origins lie in the serene village of Mukhonje, nestled within Kakamega County. From these modest beginnings, fueled by fervent prayer and a deep desire to serve, the ministry has miraculously blossomed into a significant and influential spiritual force. Its impact now resonates through countless lives across the breadth of Western Kenya, and its compassionate outreach programs are prayerfully extending their healing touch far beyond these initial borders, reaching hearts and communities with the transformative love of Christ.

At the very heart of our existence lies a sacred and core mission: to passionately and effectively spread the life-changing Gospel of Jesus Christ, to diligently nurture believers in their spiritual walk, and to actively and tangibly demonstrate God's boundless love through impactful, holistic community service. We are deeply and unreservedly committed to fostering spiritual growth, promoting holistic well-being that encompasses mind, body, and spirit, and empowering individuals from all walks of life to discover and live out their purposeful, faith-filled lives. This digital sanctuary you are visiting is a deliberate and prayerful extension of our unwavering commitment to connect, share, inspire, and grow together as a unified body in Christ Jesus.

Our Pillars of Ministry & Compassionate Outreach

Serving God, Serving Humanity with Unwavering Dedication: We invite you to explore the vibrant heart of Nehemia Ministry Kenya. Delve into our diverse and profoundly impactful programs, each meticulously designed and prayerfully implemented to uplift spirits, heal bodies and souls, educate minds, and empower communities for lasting transformation.

Kingdom Faith-Based Mission Hospital

Our hospital stands as a sanctuary of healing, offering compassionate, patient-centered, and community-focused healthcare. We seamlessly integrate unwavering faith with professional medical services of the highest standard. Our dedicated team is committed to addressing not only physical ailments but also attending to the spiritual and emotional needs of each individual, providing holistic care with utmost dedication, love, and respect. We strive to be a beacon of hope and health for all who seek our services.

Explore Our Healing Mission
Matco Training Institute – Bible School

Strategically located in Malaha, our dynamic Bible school and leadership institute serves as a crucible for spiritual formation and ministerial preparation. We are passionately committed to equipping future clergy, dedicated church workers, and empowered lay leaders with profound Biblical literacy, robust theological understanding, practical ministry skills, and a fervent missional zeal. Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to mold servant leaders who will impact their communities for Christ.

Discover Theological Education
New Promise Home – Orphanage

New Promise Home is a warm, nurturing sanctuary situated in Lwandeti, specifically established for orphaned and vulnerable children. We provide comprehensive, Christ-centered care that encompasses nutritious food, secure shelter, quality education, vital spiritual guidance, and dedicated mentorship. Our aim is to create a loving, family-like environment where every child feels valued, supported, and empowered to reach their God-given potential, growing into responsible and faith-filled individuals.

Our Ever-Expanding Family: The Nehemia Ministry Church Network Across Kenya

With hearts full of gratitude, we are joyfully extending God's kingdom, one precious community at a time. Nehemia Ministry is profoundly blessed to have a rapidly growing and vibrant family of churches, each passionately serving various regions across the beautiful nation of Kenya, sharing the light and love of Christ with all.

Nehemia Ministry Nairobi Citadel

Our dynamic urban spiritual center, strategically positioned for impactful worship, extensive outreach initiatives, and transformative community programs right in the bustling heart of Kenya's capital city. We are passionately dedicated to reaching out with the unconditional love of Christ to city dwellers from every conceivable walk of life, offering hope and spiritual nourishment.

Nehemia Ministry Mombasa Lighthouse

A radiant beacon of enduring hope and unwavering faith shining brightly on the picturesque Kenyan coast. Our Mombasa branch is wholeheartedly dedicated to serving the diverse coastal communities with rich spiritual nourishment, practical social support programs, and a message of God's unfailing love and grace for all.

Nehemia Ministry Kisumu Lakeside Sanctuary

Faithfully serving the vibrant lakeside city of Kisumu and its surrounding environs, our Kisumu church places a strong emphasis on intentional discipleship, comprehensive youth empowerment programs, and sustainable community development projects, all aimed at reflecting Christ's love and transforming lives from the inside out.

Nehemia Ministry Nakuru Rift Valley Assembly

Steadfastly growing in faith and impact within the fertile grounds of the Rift Valley. Our Nakuru assembly is a dynamic and welcoming fellowship, deeply committed to fervent evangelism, holistic ministry that addresses both spiritual and physical needs, and building strong Christian families throughout the region.

Nehemia Ministry Eldoret Highlands Tabernacle

A thriving and rapidly expanding congregation situated in the agriculturally rich North Rift region. This church is particularly focused on strengthening family ministries, providing robust leadership training for emerging leaders, and actively reaching out to the surrounding communities with the life-transforming Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Headquarters - Mukhonje, Kakamega: The Genesis

The very heart and soul from which our entire ministry was birthed and continues to prayerfully grow and expand under God's guidance. Our cherished mother church in Mukhonje village remains the central hub for spiritual leadership, ministry coordination, and regional outreach, nurturing the vision that God first planted.
