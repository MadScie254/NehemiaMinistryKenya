A Cordial Welcome

From the Shepherds of This Flock

"The grace of the Lord Jesus Christ, and the love of God, and the communion of the Holy Spirit be with you all. Amen." (2 Corinthians 13:14)

With hearts overflowing with gratitude and immense joy, we, Bishop David Walukhu and Reverend Selina Walukhu, extend our warmest and most sincere welcome to you as you explore the digital sanctuary of Nehemia Ministry Kenya. It is our profound prayer that as you navigate these pages, you will not only discover information but also encounter the tangible presence and boundless love of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.

Our ministry, born from a divine calling and nurtured by unwavering faith, is dedicated to fostering a vibrant, Christ-centered community where every soul feels valued, empowered, and drawn closer to God. We passionately believe in the transformative power of the Gospel to heal, restore, and inspire individuals to live lives of purpose, service, and deep spiritual fulfillment.

Whether you are seeking profound spiritual guidance to navigate life's complexities, a welcoming church family to share your journey of faith, a place to engage in uplifting worship that stirs the soul, or opportunities to serve and make a meaningful difference in the lives of others, we fervently believe you will find a spiritual home and a source of divine encouragement here at Nehemia Ministry. We invite you to delve into the various facets of our ministry, learn about our diverse programs, and feel the warmth and spirit of our loving and ever-growing congregation.

The Genesis of a Vision

Meet Our Esteemed Founders

Bishop David Walukhu & Reverend Selina Walukhu

Visionaries, Shepherds, and Servants of God

The journey of Nehemia Ministry Kenya is inextricably woven with the lives, faith, and unwavering dedication of its founders, Bishop David Walukhu and his beloved wife, Reverend Selina Walukhu. Their story is one of profound obedience to God's call, a relentless pursuit of spiritual excellence, and an unyielding commitment to serving humanity with the compassionate heart of Christ. From the ministry's humble inception in 2006 in the serene village of Mukhonje, Kakamega County, Bishop David and Reverend Selina have been the guiding lights, nurturing a small fellowship into a beacon of hope and spiritual vitality that now touches thousands of lives across Kenya and beyond.

Bishop David Walukhu, a man of profound wisdom and deep theological insight, received the divine mandate for Nehemia Ministry after many years of prayerful seeking and dedicated service in other capacities. His leadership is characterized by a powerful anointing in teaching the Word of God with clarity and conviction, coupled with a shepherd's heart that genuinely cares for the spiritual and physical well-being of his flock. His visionary guidance has been instrumental in expanding the ministry's reach, establishing crucial community services such as the Kingdom Faith Based Hospital and the New Promise Home, which stand as testaments to his commitment to holistic ministry.

Reverend Selina Walukhu, a woman of exceptional grace, strength, and fervent prayer, stands as a pillar of support and a dynamic spiritual force alongside her husband. Her passion for empowering women, nurturing children, and fostering a spirit of unity and love within the church community is palpable in every aspect of her ministry. Reverend Selina's leadership in various women's initiatives, her compassionate oversight of the orphanage, and her powerful intercessory prayers have been vital to the spiritual atmosphere and the compassionate outreach of Nehemia Ministry. Her gentle yet firm guidance has inspired countless individuals to discover their God-given potential and to serve with joy and dedication.

Together, Bishop David and Reverend Selina Walukhu embody the spirit of servant leadership. Their lives are a living testimony to the faithfulness of God and the transformative power of a life wholly surrendered to His will. They have weathered storms, celebrated victories, and consistently prioritized the spiritual growth of their congregation and the betterment of their community above personal comfort or acclaim. Their shared vision is not merely to build a large church, but to cultivate a family of believers deeply rooted in biblical truth, actively engaged in disciple-making, and passionately extending God's kingdom on earth. Their legacy is one of faith, resilience, and an enduring love for God and His people, inspiring generations to come to live purposefully for Christ.

Their commitment extends beyond the pulpit, deeply embedding them within the community fabric. They have championed educational initiatives through the Malaha Technical College, understanding that empowering minds is as crucial as nurturing spirits. Their hands-on involvement in disaster relief, community development projects, and advocacy for the marginalized speaks volumes of their practical application of faith. The Walukhus are not just spiritual leaders; they are community builders, mentors, and a source of unwavering hope for many who have found solace and strength through their ministry. Their home is often an extension of the church, open to those in need of counsel, prayer, or simply a listening ear, reflecting the true essence of pastoral care that defines their lifelong calling.

Our Commitment to Service

Holistic Ministry Programs

At Nehemia Ministry, we believe in serving both the spiritual and physical needs of our community. Our diverse programs and services are designed to nurture the whole person, fostering growth in faith, education, health, and social well-being.

Kingdom Faith Based Hospital

A state-of-the-art healthcare facility providing quality medical services to our community. Our services include:

  • Primary Healthcare Services
  • Maternal and Child Health
  • HIV/AIDS Care and Support
  • Health Education Programs

New Promise Home

A loving sanctuary providing care, education, and spiritual guidance to orphaned and vulnerable children. Our programs include:

  • Residential Care and Support
  • Quality Education Access
  • Psychosocial Support
  • Life Skills Training

Malaha Technical College

A comprehensive institution combining technical education with theological studies. Our curriculum focuses on:

  • Basic Technical Courses
  • Theological Studies
  • Biblical Leadership Training
  • Practical Ministry Skills

Nehemia Training Institute

Empowering individuals through quality education and vocational training. Our offerings include:

  • Vocational Skills Training
  • Leadership Development
  • Entrepreneurship Programs
  • Biblical Studies

Community Development

Sustainable initiatives that empower and uplift our community. Our projects include:

  • Agricultural Programs
  • Water and Sanitation
  • Economic Empowerment
  • Environmental Conservation

Youth Ministry

Engaging and empowering young people through various programs:

  • Youth Leadership Training
  • Career Guidance
  • Sports and Recreation
  • Annual Youth Conferences

Women's Ministry

Empowering women through spiritual and practical support:

  • Bible Study Groups
  • Skills Development
  • Support Groups
  • Entrepreneurship Training

Lives Transformed by Grace

Member since 2015

Nehemia Ministry is more than a church; it's a life-transforming family that showed me the true meaning of God's grace and provision. I am eternally grateful for their unwavering support and the profound impact they've had on my entire family's spiritual and material well-being."

- Jane M.

Peter K., Youth Leader

Involved since 2018

The annual youth conferences are always a highlight, filled with powerful messages and an incredible sense of fellowship. Thanks to the training and spiritual grounding from Nehemia, I am now a youth leader myself, passionate about guiding other young people towards a life in Christ. This ministry doesn't just preach; it actively invests in the next generation, building a legacy of faith and service. The impact is immeasurable, creating ripples of positive change throughout our community."

- Peter K.

Esther A., Community Health Worker

Beneficiary & Volunteer

"When my child fell seriously ill, we had little hope and even fewer resources. The Nehemia Medical Center was a Godsend. The care, compassion, and professionalism of the staff were exceptional, and they treated us with such dignity. It wasn't just about medical treatment; the spiritual support and prayers from the ministry team gave us immense strength during a very dark period. After my child recovered, I felt a strong calling to give back. I now volunteer at the clinic and participate in the ministry's community outreach programs. Seeing firsthand the tangible impact of Nehemia Ministry from providing healthcare to feeding the hungry and caring for orphans has deepened my faith profoundly. This is truly a ministry that lives out the Gospel, demonstrating Christ's love in action every single day. Their holistic approach to well-being is a blessing to our entire region."

- Esther A.
