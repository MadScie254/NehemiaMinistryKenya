The Story of His Faithfulness

Our Sacred Journey: A Chronicle of Faith, Service, and Divine Providence

From a humble seed of a divine calling planted in the hearts of our founders, Nehemia Ministry has blossomed into a sprawling tree of life, offering spiritual shelter, healing, and hope to thousands. This is our story.

The Vessels of the Vision

An In-Depth Look at Our Beloved Founders

Bishop David & Reverend Selina Walukhu

"For we are God’s handiwork, created in Christ Jesus to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do." (Ephesians 2:10)

The narrative of Nehemia Ministry is a divine tapestry, intricately woven with the threads of faith, sacrifice, and unwavering obedience of its founders, Bishop David Walukhu and his cherished wife, Reverend Selina Walukhu. Their journey is a powerful testament to what God can accomplish through hearts fully surrendered to His will. Long before the first stone of the ministry was laid, a divine call was stirring in their spirits—a call to rebuild the walls of faith, restore hope to the brokenhearted, and establish a sanctuary where the transformative power of the Gospel could be experienced by all.

Bishop David Walukhu, a man of profound biblical insight and a shepherd's heart, spent his early years in ministry serving with distinction in various capacities, all the while sensing a deeper, more specific purpose that God had ordained for him. This period of preparation was marked by intense prayer, diligent study of the Word, and a growing burden for the spiritual and physical well-being of the people in Kakamega County. In 2006, after a season of profound spiritual encounters, the vision for Nehemia Ministry was birthed—clear, compelling, and divinely mandated. His leadership is characterized by bold, faith-filled preaching that challenges the comfortable and comforts the challenged, coupled with a strategic vision that has seen the ministry grow from a small fellowship into a holistic, community-transforming force.

Standing resolutely by his side is Reverend Selina Walukhu, a woman of extraordinary grace, fervent prayer, and immense spiritual strength. Her journey has been one of deep partnership in the Gospel, providing the essential spiritual backbone and compassionate touch that has defined the ministry's character. With a special anointing for intercession and a deep passion for empowering women and nurturing children, Reverend Selina has been the driving force behind many of the ministry's most impactful outreach programs. She has tirelessly championed the cause of the vulnerable, overseeing the New Promise Home with a mother's love and leading the women's ministry with wisdom and grace. Her life is a beautiful portrait of the Proverbs 31 woman, a pillar of strength and a wellspring of wisdom for the entire congregation.

Together, they are more than just leaders; they are spiritual parents to a generation. They have navigated immense challenges, celebrated countless miracles, and consistently placed the needs of their flock and community above their own. Their shared legacy is not built in brick and mortar, but in the thousands of transformed lives, restored families, and rekindled hopes that are the true testimony of Nehemia Ministry Kenya. Their story is a continuous unfolding of God's faithfulness, an inspiration for all who dare to dream, believe, and step out in faith to answer God's call.

The Foundation of Our Faith

What We Believe

Our ministry is built upon the unshakable foundation of God's Word. These core tenets of our faith guide our worship, our fellowship, and our service to the world.

The Holy Scriptures

We believe the Bible, in its 66 books of the Old and New Testaments, is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, the ultimate guide for faith and life.

2 Timothy 3:16-17

The Holy Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three distinct persons: the Father, the Son (Jesus Christ), and the Holy Spirit, co-equal in power and glory.

Matthew 28:19

Salvation by Grace

We believe that salvation is a free gift from God, received by grace through faith in the atoning sacrifice of Jesus Christ, not by human works or merit.

Ephesians 2:8-9

The Person of Jesus Christ

We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, in His virgin birth, His sinless life, His miracles, His vicarious and atoning death, His bodily resurrection, and His ascension to the right hand of the Father.

John 1:1, 14

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, whose indwelling enables believers to live a godly life, empowers them for service, and endows them with spiritual gifts.

Acts 1:8

The Church & Great Commission

We believe in the spiritual unity of all believers in our Lord Jesus Christ, who form the Church. We are mandated by the Great Commission to make disciples of all nations.

Matthew 28:19-20

A Journey Marked by Grace

Our Ministry Milestones

2006: The Divine Spark

The Genesis of a Movement

Following a period of intense prayer and seeking, Bishop David Walukhu receives the divine vision for Nehemia Ministry. A small, passionate group begins meeting for fellowship in Mukhonje, laying the spiritual foundation for all that was to come.

2010: A Haven of Hope

New Promise Home is Born

With a deep burden for the region's vulnerable children, the ministry establishes the New Promise Home. This wasn't just a building, but a loving sanctuary dedicated to providing care, education, and spiritual nurturing to orphans.

2015: Healing Hands Extended

Kingdom Faith Based Hospital Opens

Recognizing the critical need for accessible healthcare, the ministry undertakes its most ambitious project yet. The Kingdom Faith Based Hospital is inaugurated, offering quality, compassionate medical care as a tangible expression of Christ's love.

2018: Empowering the Next Generation

Launch of Malaha Technical College

To equip young people with both spiritual truth and practical skills, the Malaha Technical College is launched. This initiative combines theological studies with vocational training, empowering students for life and ministry.

2022: Expanding the Digital Tent

Global Online Outreach

Embracing technology for the Gospel, the ministry launches a comprehensive website with sermon archives, online giving, and innovative AI tools, extending its reach far beyond the borders of Kenya to a global audience.

Today & Beyond: The Vision Continues

Pressing Toward the Mark

Nehemia Ministry continues to grow, driven by the same founding vision: to be a beacon of faith, hope, and love. We are passionately pursuing new avenues of service and discipleship, trusting God for an even greater impact in the years to come.
